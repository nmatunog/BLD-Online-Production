BLD Cebu Online Portal — Member Guide

Quick steps to Sign up, Sign in, and Check in to Community Worship, WSC, and other events.

For members Chatbot + Mobile Number QR Code Ready Print-Friendly
1

Sign up with Chatbot (Mobile)

  1. Open the Portal and go to the Register / Sign Up page.
  2. Choose Sign up with Chatbot / Chatbot Assistant.
  3. When asked, choose Mobile number as your sign-up method.
  4. Enter your mobile number (e.g. 09XX XXX XXXX).
  5. Create a password (min. 6 characters) and remember it.
  6. Provide details the chatbot asks for: name, city, encounter type (ME/SE/YE/SPE), class number.
  7. When the chatbot says “Registration successful”, your account is ready.

Tip: You may also sign up with your email instead of mobile, if you prefer.

2

Sign in to the Portal

  1. From the Portal home, open the Login page.
  2. Sign in with either:
    • Mobile number + password, or
    • Email + password (if you used email to register).
  3. Click or tap Sign in.
  4. When your details are correct, you’ll go to your Dashboard.
  5. Optional: on the Login page, you can use the Chatbot and choose “Sign in” instead of the form.
  6. Optional: if you have a member QR code, scan it on the login screen and then enter your password.
3

Check in to an event

  1. Sign in to the Portal (see Step 2).
  2. Open Self Check-in (members) or Check-in (staff).
  3. Select the event (e.g. Community Worship or Word Sharing Circle). The nearest / today’s event usually appears at the top.
  4. Choose how to check in:
    • Scan member QR code: allow camera, point at your QR (from profile or printed ID), wait for success.
    • Use Check-in Chatbot: open the chatbot on the Self Check-in page, confirm the event and your identity, then wait for the confirmation message.
  5. Once you see a success / “You are checked in” message, you’re done.

When you can check in: from 2 hours before the event starts until 2 hours after it ends.
For weekly events (like Community Worship / WSC), you can also check in within 7 days after the event.

One-glance summary (for infographic)

Sign up
Open Portal → Register → Chatbot → choose Mobile number → enter phone & password → give name, city, encounter, class → chatbot confirms “Registration successful”.
Sign in
Open Portal → Login → enter mobile (or email) + password → Sign in → see your Dashboard. Chatbot and QR login are also available from the Login page.
Check in
Sign in → Self Check-in → select event (e.g. Community Worship / WSC) → scan member QR or use Check-in Chatbot → wait for success message → you’re checked in.
