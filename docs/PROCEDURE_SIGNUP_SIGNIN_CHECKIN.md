# BLD Cebu Online Portal — Member Procedure

**Quick guide: Sign up (Chatbot + mobile), Sign in, and Check in to events**

*Use this as a reference or convert into an infographic.*

---

## 1. Sign up using the Chatbot Assistant (Mobile Number)

1. Open the **Portal** in your browser (phone or computer).
2. Go to the **Sign up / Register** page.
3. Choose **“Sign up with Chatbot”** or **“Use Chatbot Assistant”**.
4. In the chat, when asked how you want to sign up, choose **Mobile number** (or say you want to use your phone number).
5. Enter your **mobile number** when the chatbot asks (e.g. 09XX XXX XXXX).
6. Create a **password** (at least 6 characters) when asked. Remember it for signing in.
7. Provide the details the chatbot asks for:
   - First name, last name  
   - City  
   - Encounter type (e.g. ME, SE, YE)  
   - Class number  
8. Finish the conversation. When the chatbot confirms **“Registration successful”**, your account is created.
9. You can then **Sign in** with your mobile number and password.

**Tip:** You can also sign up using **email** instead of mobile if you prefer.

---

## 2. Signing in

1. Open the **Portal** and go to the **Login** page.
2. Choose one:
   - **Sign in with mobile number:** Enter your mobile number and password.  
   - **Sign in with email:** Enter your email and password (if you used email when signing up).
3. Enter your **password**.
4. Tap or click **Sign in**.
5. If details are correct, you are taken to your **Dashboard**.

**Other option:** On the Login page you can open the **Chatbot** and choose **“Sign in”** — then enter your mobile number (or email) and password in the chat.

**QR option:** If you have your member QR code, you can scan it on the Login page and then enter your password to sign in.

---

## 3. Checking in to an event (e.g. Community Worship or WSC)

1. **Sign in** to the Portal (see Section 2).
2. In the menu, go to **Self Check-in** (or **Check-in** if you are staff).
3. **Select the event** (e.g. “Community Worship” or “Word Sharing Circle”) from the dropdown. The **nearest or today’s event** is usually at the top.
4. Choose how to check in:
   - **Scan your member QR code**  
     - Allow camera access if asked.  
     - Point your phone camera at your member QR code (from your profile or printed ID).  
     - When it’s recognized, you’re checked in.
   - **Or use the Chatbot**  
     - Open the check-in chatbot in the Self Check-in page.  
     - Follow the prompts (e.g. confirm event, confirm identity).  
     - The chatbot will confirm when you’re checked in.
5. When check-in is successful, you’ll see a **success message** or confirmation. You’re done.

**When you can check in:** You can check in from **2 hours before** the event start until **2 hours after** the event end (e.g. for 7:00 PM Community Worship, from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM). For recurring events (e.g. weekly WSC), you can also check in within **7 days after** the event.

**Tip:** If you don’t see your event, make sure you’ve selected the right date (today’s or this week’s event is shown first).
